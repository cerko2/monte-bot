15.2.5 Line Styles

Line styles are specified by the following properties:

linestyle
May be one of
"-"
Solid lines.
"--"
Dashed lines.
":"
Points.
"-."
A dash-dot line.

linewidth
A number specifying the width of the line. The default is 1. A value of 2 is twice as wide as the default, etc.
